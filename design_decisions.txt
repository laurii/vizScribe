Options for graphical display of transcript
  * horizontal series of rectangles for each line spoken equally spaced,
    equal width, equal height.
    ADVANTAGES:
      - effective use of space. 
      - MagicLens-type zooming is easier and less confusing.
    DISADVANTAGES: 
      - pauses in conversation are not shown
      - difficult to obtain a temporal correspondence to timeline
  * Horizontal series of rectangles for each line spoken, width
    proportional to timespan of line spoken, equal height.
